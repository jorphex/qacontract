How to take the hill

Learn to read and write a smart contract by playing. Find the question, inspect public state, submit a transaction, and verify the result on Basescan.

01

Find the question

Open Read Contract on Basescan and query prompt().

02

Take a shot

Open Write Contract, connect your wallet, then call shoot(answer). The answer must match exactly, including capitalization.

03

Use ammo wisely

You get limited shots. The visible holder is not necessarily winning; only correct hold time is banked, and banked results stay hidden.

04

Settle the game

After the live deadline, anyone may call finalize(). The contract pays the best banked result.
