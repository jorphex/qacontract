How It Works

  • Call shoot(answer) to capture the hill. Every address has a limited number of shots.
  • Each capture resets the visible prize curve to the floor.
  • Only time held with the correct answer banks toward the final prize.
  • Recapturing with the correct answer resumes your hidden bank from where it left off.
  • The last correct reign before the deadline wins the banked prize.
  • If no correct reign exists at the end, the creator can claw back the pool.
